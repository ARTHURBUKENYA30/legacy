Vision & Mission

Vision: To ignite greatness and define a lasting legacy through world-class squash education, competition, and character building.

Mission: To nurture disciplined, elite African athletes by providing modern facilities, capacity building, professional coaching, and global exposure.

  • Promoting integrity, inclusion, and anti-doping compliance.

  • Strengthening Uganda’s presence in global squash.
