Vision & Mission

Vision: Ignite greatness and define lasting legacy through world-class squash education and character development.

Mission: Develop elite African athletes through professional coaching, safeguarding, global exposure, and structured competition.

Constitution Summary

Legal Status: Registered in Kampala under Companies Act.

Objectives:

  • Talent identification & structured athlete development.
  • National & international squash competitions.
  • Integrity, safeguarding & anti-doping compliance.
  • Partnerships with schools, sponsors & federations.
  • Promote Uganda globally through squash excellence.

Governance: Managed by Board of Directors.

Finance: Transparent accounting & annual audits.

Adopted: 2025 – Kampala.
