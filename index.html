Vision & Mission

Vision: To ignite greatness and define a lasting legacy through world-class squash education, competition, and character building.

Mission: To nurture disciplined, elite African athletes by providing modern facilities, capacity building, professional coaching, and global exposure.

Constitution Summary

Legal Foundation: Registered in Kampala under the Companies Act...

  • Talent identification and structured training.
  • Hosting national & international squash events.
  • Promoting integrity, inclusion, and anti-doping compliance.
  • Partnering with schools, sponsors, and sports federations.
  • Strengthening Uganda’s presence in global squash.

Governance Structure: Managed by a Board of Directors...

Founders: Ivan Wasswa – Director of Athlete Development; Paul Kasirye Kadoma – Director of Technical Programs.

Co-Founder: Julian Ainomugisha - Seed one Uganda female squash player and qualified quantity surveyor with safeguarding skills and PR!

Finance & Compliance: Transparent accounting, annual audits...
